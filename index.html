Basic Information

1.Choose radius from controls and then click start.
2.Each square signifies area of 1cm2.
3.To calculate area of circle we will see number of squares filled by circle.
4.Ignore portions of the area that are less than half a square.
5.If more than half of a square is in a region, just count it as one square.
6. If exactly half the square is counted, take its area as 1/2sq unit.
7.The rotating line and the yellow region gives the area covered by the circle.
8.To change radius click stop than change radius and then click start.
